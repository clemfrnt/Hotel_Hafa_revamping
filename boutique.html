Shop at our Boutique

« Live what you love »

Enormous hearts decorate this beautiful art & jewelry shop.  Here you will encounter art & decorative pieces from many different regions of Mexico, as well as stunning yet casual pearl jewelry designed by the shop’s owner, Marina Garcia. She has combined leather & pearls to create a type of jewelry that is easy to wear, that passes on love and thrill for life to those who wear them. Pearls will always be the mystery of those who wear them.
